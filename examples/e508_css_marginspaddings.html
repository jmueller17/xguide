e508 - CSS - Margins, Paddings, Borders

1.
 Margin
 

Margins define the space between a selected element and its neighbouring, external elements.

 
<p style="margin:30px 10px 60px 10px">abe cure</p>

abe cure. margin values are top, right, bottom left and apply from the paragraph tag to the table cell.

  <span style="margin-left:30px; margin-right:30px">abe ....</span> abe cure

abe here applies span cure abe cure. Note for <span> the margin-bottom/top have no effect.

 



2.
 Padding

Padding defines the spacing between a selected element and its contents.

 
  <p style="padding:30px 10px 60px 10px" border:1px solid red;>abe cure ...</p>

To visualize the padding, the paragraph received a red border.

 

3.
 Border

Much of padding and margins doesn't make sense without indicating the border.

 
  <p style="border-color:10px">abe cure</p>

abe cure
  <p style="border-style:dashed">abe cure</p>

abe cure
  <p style="border-width:10px; border-style:solid">abe..</p>

abe cure
